    No need to always be on a GPU!

    Every Jarvis instance can be scaled on the fly. No need to stick to a GPU instance if you don't need it. Behind the scenes, we do this by shifting your machine's disk over to a different instance type, briefly restarting it.

    Scaling an instance

    In the Brev console, go to your instance settings and under hardware, you'll see a slider to scale your instance. (At the moment, this only works for non-SPOT instances. We are working on a fix for SPOT instances so stay tuned 🤙)
